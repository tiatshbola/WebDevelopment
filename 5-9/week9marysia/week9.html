Computer Love

A story by Tia Tshabola

The Story Starts In 2024

I bought my first laptop with my first bursary. Little did I know this was going to be the beginning of an amazing creative journey.

The Honeymoon

We did everything together, sleep, create, and even shower! (wait..)

My First Win

That same year, I entered a hackathon, and with this same very computer doing my prototyping, I won! He's amazing.

Computer in Full Use

There was no doubt I needed a laptop like this. I got all my work done sufficiently for university — very fast and hassle free!

Uh Oh

A problem… as I started to get into heavier 3D rendering, video jockeying and editing, He was no longer holding up as well.

The Divorce

The installations I was doing had gotten to a point where the he no longer functioned well enough. He served many projects, but after 2 years his time was up.

The Replacement

A tall, dark and handsome man came into town — the Lenovo Legion 7. New and upgraded. He is going to take me to the next level.

But I'll never forget my first love.

You don't need big starts to get ahead. Start small and upgrade later!
